Instructions

In this game, you will estimate a value in each round. There are 15 rounds in which you will give your estimation. In each round, the correct value results from the determinants A, B, C, D and E. The determinants A,B,C and D will be displayed to you in each round. The determinant E is a random number determined for each period and takes on a value between -25 and 25; you do not know this number.

The formula to calculate the value is:
value = 2*A-B-0.5*C+D^2+E

This formula is valid for every round of the game. As soon as all players have submitted their estimation at the end of each round, the estimations of all the other players will be displayed, as well as the resulting average estimation. Starting from the second round, you will also have to estimate whether the value will be higher or lower than the average estimation of the preceding round. In each round, you will have 30 s to enter your estimation and click on NEXT to confirm it. Please note:If you do not enter a number within these 30 seconds and confirm it with NEXT, your payment in the corresponding round will be 0 Euros.

Your PAYOFF is as follows:
max(0, 0.50 - 0.01 * |your estimation - value|) where || represents absolute value
